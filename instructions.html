Egg-Gen Instructions

Made By Rayden Zhang

To play Egg Generator, press E to make an egg.
There are different kinds of eggs, which are brown eggs, white eggs, gold eggs,
diamond eggs, and special eggs. Brown and white eggs have a 50/50 chance. Gold
eggs have a 1% chance of spawning. Diamond eggs have a a 0.1% chance of
spawning, and special eggs have a one out of 5000 chance
of spawning. (0.02%)! You'll know what special eggs are when you see them.

Your data, eggs, promo codes and all the other stuff will be saved automatically.

To wipe your data, click the "Wipe Data" button.
